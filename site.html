The world is a dangerous and mysterious place. Before the evolution of science, men created myths to explain that which they saw around them. The Greeks in particular created various deities for each and every thing. Every tree was home to a nymph. Each river has its own mighty god. One god, Pricus, is actually the father of Sea Goats.
